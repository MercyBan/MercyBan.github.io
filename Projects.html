Virtual Pets Amok

This project was a console based game which players can rescue pets from a shelter and adopt it.
The pet can be organic or cyborg crabs. Each pet had a different feeding method and multiple.
characteristics affected the status of the crab’s health and hunger level, as well boredom.
We implemented the tick method each time an activity was called and the crabs health.

High Street Hospital

A console application to replicate a hospital’s information management system.
The applications track all medical staff and workers as well as incoming patients and what treatment.
